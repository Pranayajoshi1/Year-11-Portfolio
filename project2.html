Functionality Usability Intellectual property

Functionality

Functionality refers to the practical aspects or capabilities of a design or object. In the context of game it help to help to locate errors, glitches, and bugs that may negatively affect the player experience. Determining whether the application is working according to the specifications.

I addressed this by:

  • Conduct extensive testing to identify and fix bugs and glitches before release.
  • Use clear and simple text commands that players can easily understand and remember.
  • Provide helpful tutorials or a guide at the beginning of the game to familiarize players with the mechanics and commands.

Usability

Usability refers to ensuring our game is easy to use, the ease with which people can become efficient in the usage, operation, and interaction of a product or system

I addressed this by:

  • Choose fonts that are easy to read and appropriate for the medieval theme
  • Provide immediate and clear feedback for player inputs, confirming actions or explaining errors (e.g., "You can't go that way" or "The key has been added to your inventory").

Intellectual Property

Intellectual Property (IP) refers to creations of the mind like stories, characters, artwork, music, and inventions. It gives creators legal rights to protect their work from being used without their permission.

I addressed this by:

  • The original story, characters, and dialogue of the game are protected by copyright, preventing others from copying or distributing these elements without permission.
  • Unique names, titles, and logos associated with the game can be trademarked to prevent others from using similar names or symbols that could confuse consumers.

Accessibility

ccessibility in the context of game design refers to the practice of making a game usable and enjoyable by as many people as possible, including those with disabilities or specific needs.

I addressed this by:

  • Use clear and straightforward language in the game text and instructions.
  • Implement a robust help system and provide hints to assist players who may get stuck
